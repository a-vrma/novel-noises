Novel Noises

Music for reading!

NovelNoises is a database of music paired with books. Many people live exceedingly busy lives with little time to relax at home with a book. Some are forced to read during their loud lunch hour or crowded commute. Instead of using music to block out unwanted noises while reading, why not use music to complement the reading experience? Yeet. That's where NovelNoises comes in. Just search our database for a book and pick an album or playlist from the hundreds of recommendations submitted by users.
